Curiosity Rover's Martian Photo Album

Embark on a visual journey with Curiosity, NASA's most famous Mars rover, as it explores the Red Planet! By entering a Martian Sol (a day on Mars), you can discover stunning photos captured by Curiosity. Sol starts from 0, marking the rover's grand touchdown on Mars, and spans to its latest adventures. Fancy seeing what Curiosity was up to on its 1000th day? Just type in Sol 1000 and prepare to be amazed by the snapshots of Martian landscapes and curiosities uncovered by this intrepid explorer.
