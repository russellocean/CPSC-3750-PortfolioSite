About StellarScape

StellarScape is a web application that aims to bring the wonders of space exploration closer to you. We leverage NASA's powerful APIs to provide you with stunning imagery and insightful data from various missions, including the Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) and the Mars Rover missions.

Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD)

One of the most popular websites at NASA is the Astronomy Picture of the Day. This website showcases a different image or video from the cosmos every day, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer. The APOD API allows us to fetch and display these captivating images and descriptions, providing you with a daily dose of cosmic wonder.

Mars Rover Photos

NASA's Curiosity, Opportunity, and Spirit rovers have been exploring the Red Planet, capturing breathtaking images and gathering valuable data. The Mars Rover Photos API allows us to access and showcase these incredible images, organized by the Martian sol (day) on which they were taken. Explore the Martian landscapes through the eyes of these intrepid rovers and witness the marvels of our neighboring planet.

At StellarScape, we are passionate about space exploration and committed to sharing the wonders of the cosmos with you. We hope that our application will inspire you to learn more about the universe and appreciate the incredible achievements of NASA and the scientific community.

All data and imagery provided by StellarScape are sourced from NASA's APIs, which are publicly available for educational and non-commercial purposes. For more information about NASA's APIs, please visit api.nasa.gov.
